Create your collections

Tell us which platforms you play on and which games you own for each. Take pride in you game collection and share them with the world!

Create or register for a meetup

Browse and register for meetups in your area. Feel free to create one if there are none nearby. Although remember the more people at a meetup, the better, so try not spread out too far!

Browse collections of attendees

After registering for an event, start browsing the collections of everyone else attending. Find a game you like? Organise a swap before you arrive to get the most out of the event.

Build your Swapsy score

With every swap or completed achievement, you increase your Swapsy score. The higher your Swapsy score the more trustworthy you appear for online swaps. Try attending as many meetups as possible to boost your score!
